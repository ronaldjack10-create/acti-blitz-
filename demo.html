The mechanism

The four states that decide your demo.

ACTI-BLITZ runs all four detectors continuously during a live call. When a state surfaces, the rep sees what the buyer is actually doing. And the response that fits the moment.

01 · STATE

Unstable Awareness

The buyer can feel the problem but can't name it.

Signal

Vague dissatisfaction. "Things feel chaotic." "I don't know what we're missing." Buyer narrates symptoms instead of the root cause.

A buyer who can't name the problem can't commit to the solution. The deal goes quiet after the demo.

02 · STATE

Internal Conflict

Two priorities pull the buyer in opposite directions.

Signal

"I love it, but my team…" "It's a great fit, but timing…" Approval and resistance held in the same sentence.

Internal Conflict is not an objection. The buyer is arguing with themselves. Pitch harder and you lose them.

03 · STATE

Decision Compression

The buyer has run out of room to decide. Risk is louder than opportunity.

Signal

Rapid-fire questions about edge cases. Tone shift mid-call. "What if it doesn't work?" New stakeholders surfacing late.

Compression is a fast-burning fuse. Miss the window and the deal stalls into no-decision.

04 · STATE

Self-Protection Mode

The buyer is protecting themselves from a bad bet. Not evaluating yours.

Signal

Sudden need for "alignment with stakeholders." Asks for written proposals "to circulate." The calendar gets harder to find.

This is where great demos become quiet inboxes. The buyer isn't stalling. They're hiding from the decision.
