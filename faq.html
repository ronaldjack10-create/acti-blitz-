01 · The Product

What exactly is ACTI-BLITZ™ and what does it do during a sales call?

ACTI-BLITZ™ is a real-time buyer psychology detection platform for high-ticket B2B sales calls. While your rep is on the call, the system listens to the conversation and detects which of four psychological states the buyer is in: Unstable Awareness, Internal Conflict, Decision Compression, or Self-Protection Mode.

When a state is detected, your rep gets the exact closing language tuned to where the buyer's mind actually is — not a generic script, not a post-call summary, not a probability score. The signal arrives while the deal is still live.

02 · Differentiation

How is this different from Gong, Chorus, or other call intelligence tools?

Gong, Chorus, and the broader call-intelligence category record the call and tell you what happened after the call is over. That's coaching infrastructure. It's valuable — but the deal is already won or lost by the time the analysis arrives.

ACTI-BLITZ™ operates in the live conversation. It's the perception layer — a separate category from post-call analytics. The closest competitor framing: Supersonik replaces the rep. Cluely puppeteers the rep. ACTI-BLITZ makes the rep dangerous.

03 · The Framework

What are the four buyer states and where did they come from?

The four states are Unstable Awareness, Internal Conflict, Decision Compression, and Self-Protection Mode. Each describes a distinct psychological position a buyer can occupy mid-call, and each has a different correct response.

They came from Ron Magic Jackson's 20 years of high-ticket B2B sales — codified into a methodology called The Art of Connecting and three published books before becoming the spine of ACTI-BLITZ™. The full taxonomy is in the architecture brief, which Design Partners receive on NDA.

07 · Integration

How does ACTI-BLITZ™ work with my existing stack — Zoom, Salesforce, Slack?

Phase 1 is delivered as a Chrome extension that runs alongside your existing call stack (Zoom, Google Meet, Teams). It does not require ripping out Gong, replacing your CRM, or changing how your reps work today. It sits on top of the call layer.

Phase 2 broadens integration into Salesforce, HubSpot, and revenue ops tooling. The full integration roadmap is in the architecture brief.

08 · Speed

How fast is the detection — is this truly real-time?

Phase 1 detection runs on roughly 30-second async transcription windows for Unstable Awareness, Internal Conflict, and Self-Protection Mode — fast enough to course-correct mid-call but not millisecond-real-time. Decision Compression alerts fire immediately, because the window to act on that signal is too short for any delay.

Phase 2 architecture reduces all four states to live signal as the underlying infrastructure matures. The Phase 1 spec is the spec Design Partners get — no overpromising.

09 · Data & Security

What happens to my call data? Where is it stored? Who has access?

Call audio passes through AssemblyAI for transcription, then through the ACTI-BLITZ™ analysis layer (Claude API + Cohere for processing, Qdrant for state-detection embeddings). Calls are not stored long-term during the Design Partner phase unless a partner explicitly opts in for product-improvement training.

Full detail — vendor stack, retention windows, access controls, deletion rights — is on the Trust & Security page. Direct security questions: info@e2active.com.

10 · Ideal Fit

Who is ACTI-BLITZ™ built for — and who is it not for?

Built for: SaaS founders selling $50K+ deals where the rep is on the call, B2B sales leaders running teams of 5–30, and revenue operators in companies between $1M and $10M ARR who feel their close rate is more variable than it should be. The pattern: high-ticket, conversation-driven, human-in-the-loop.

Not built for: low-ticket transactional sales, fully automated outbound, SDR cadence tools, or teams where reps are not on live calls. ACTI-BLITZ™ enhances closer judgment — it doesn't replace the human in the loop. The Klarna lesson applies: when you remove the human from high-stakes work, quality collapses. ACTI-BLITZ™ is built around that fact, not against it.
