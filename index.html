Section III · The 4 States

HOW IT
WORKS

The 4 buyer states you've been missing

Every B2B prospect cycles through these 4 psychological states on every call. ACTI-BLITZ™ detects which state they're in — and gives you the exact line that moves them forward.

State One

Unstable Awareness

Your prospect knows there's a problem — but they're still defining it on the call. They're testing language, sounding it out, watching how you react.

"I'm just looking right now"
"I'm not sure what we need yet"
"Tell me more about what you do"
ACTI-BLITZ™ Closing Line
"It sounds like you're still mapping the territory — that's actually the most important phase. Walk me through what's happening on a Tuesday morning when this comes up. I'll tell you whether we're a fit or not."
Why It Works

You stop pitching and start helping them name their problem. When they hear themselves describe it — they advance to State 2 on their own.

State Two

Internal Conflict

They want it. They also want what they have now. Two voices are arguing — and one wrong word from you sends them back to State 1.

"This sounds great, but…"
"I'd love to but the budget…"
"Let me think about it"
ACTI-BLITZ™ Closing Line
"There's a part of you that wants this and a part that's worried. Both are right. Let's name the worry so we can deal with it directly — what's the specific concern?"
Why It Works

You acknowledge both sides without pushing. The prospect feels seen, not sold to — and the conflict resolves toward you.

State Three

Decision Compression

The window is open. They're ready to move — but it's a 30-second window. Miss it and they cool down. Most reps talk right through it.

"OK so what do we do next?"
"How fast can we get started?"
"What's the simplest way to do this?"
ACTI-BLITZ™ Closing Line
"Let's lock it in right now. I can have your access set up in the next 10 minutes. Want me to walk you through the one-page agreement live?"
Why It Works

You match their energy with immediate action. ACTI-BLITZ™ flags this state in real-time — the moment it appears, you stop pitching and start closing.

State Four

Self-Protection Mode

They're about to say yes — then fear hits harder than logic. One wrong move here and a won deal evaporates in seconds.

"Actually… can I ask you something?"
"I'm putting my reputation on the line"
"Maybe I should wait until next quarter"
ACTI-BLITZ™ Closing Line
"I'm glad you brought that up — that fear means you care about getting this right. Here's what I can tell you: we have a 30-day performance guarantee. If you're not seeing measurable improvement in week three, you walk away. Your reputation isn't on the line. Mine is."
Why It Works

You validate the fear and transfer the risk from them to you. When they feel protected — they move forward. Every time.
